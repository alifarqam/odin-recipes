Karahi

Description

Chicken Karahi, also called Kadai Chicken, is a chicken curry with a spicy tomato base, fragrant spices, and ginger. To finish the curry, we add plenty of green chilies and fresh cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Thin Sliced Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger & Garlic Paste
  • cumin, red spice, turmeric
  • 1KG Chicken

Steps

  1. Heat Oil and Fry onions and tomatoes
  2. Add all the spices and stir fry
  3. Add chicken
  4. Cook for 20 Mins
